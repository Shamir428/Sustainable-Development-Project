Preventive health and getting help early

Many health problems start with early signs like poor sleep, constant stress, low mood, or symptoms that do not go away. Preventive health means taking steps to stay well through healthy habits and regular check-ups, while getting help early means reaching out for support before a problem becomes more serious. For students, academic pressure and busy routines can make these signs easy to ignore, but early action can make a real difference to both physical and mental health.
