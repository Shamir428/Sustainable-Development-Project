Preventive Health: Small Actions, Better Outcomes

Preventive health is about taking small and practical steps to protect your well-being before health problems become serious. For students, busy schedules, stress, poor sleep and procrastination can lead to early warning signs of health problems being ignored or neglected. This page explores why acting early matters, how prevention supports both physical and mental health, and how students can take action to protect their health and seek support sooner.
