                      Facilities 

                      Located in the heart of London, just a short walk from bustling Oxford Street, is Regent Street gym.

                      Our gym offers cardio and weights facilities, and general floor space for stretching or free weights. We also have clean and pleasant changing, shower and locker facilities.

                      Highly qualified and experienced fitness instructors are on hand to offer advice and personal training.
