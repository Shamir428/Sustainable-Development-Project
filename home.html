Improving Health & Wellbeing

This website encourages students to take practical steps towards better physical and mental health.

Mental Health Matters

Support and open conversations help reduce stress and stigma.

  • Check in on friends
  • Use support services
  • Talk openly

Stay Active

Regular movement improves both mental and physical health.

  • Walk daily
  • Join sports
  • Take breaks

Healthy Habits

Good nutrition and sleep help maintain energy and focus.

  • Eat well
  • Stay hydrated
  • Sleep properly
