Reflection on Validation

During validation, I encountered several errors such as empty image source attributes, missing alt text, incorrect heading structure, and duplicate IDs.

These issues were resolved by adding valid image paths, ensuring all images included descriptive alt attributes, correcting heading levels, and making sure all IDs were unique.

This process improved my understanding of HTML standards and highlighted the importance of writing clean, well-structured, and accessible code.
